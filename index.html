Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Division of Applied Mathematics at Brown University. My research interests include scientific computing and numerical analysis of high-order methods for PDEs, especially as they pertain to problems in computational fluid dynamics. Presently, I am researching the use of deep neural networks to solve PDEs with my advisor, Professor Mark Ainsworth. I am a recipient of the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

I am interested in increasing the participation of historically underrepresented groups in higher mathematics. I founded the APMA Directed Reading Program which runs each fall and spring semester.

I will be defending my dissertation in April 2023!
